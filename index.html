About

Chris Trethewey is a clinical researcher working in haematology and cancer research in the UK. His work focuses on liquid biopsy approaches for monitoring blood cancers.

GitHub

ProbeScope

A command-line tool for generating per-gene coverage track plots from BAM files, overlaid on panel target BED annotations with MANE-Select exon models.

ONCOSIEVE

Pan-cancer somatic variant curation pipeline that aggregates COSMIC, GENIE, ClinVar, OncoKB, and other curated sources to build a tiered whitelist for rescuing clinically relevant low-VAF variants.

DELVE

Deep-sequencing and Evaluation of Lymphoid Variant Effects.
